## Getting Started

1xcoder is a developer portfolio platform that showcases web development projects, skills, and professional experience. It provides a comprehensive overview of a developer's work and capabilities in web development. The platform helps developers present their portfolios effectively to potential employers and clients.

## Key Features

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Project Showcase" icon="folder">
    Display your web development projects with rich descriptions, technology stacks, and live links. Each project includes detailed explanations of challenges overcome and solutions implemented.

    <Callout kind="tip">Use the project gallery to highlight your best work and demonstrate your development expertise.</Callout>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Project" icon="plus">
        Click the add project button to start showcasing your work.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload Details" icon="upload">
        Provide project title, description, technologies, and images.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish" icon="send">
        Make your project visible to potential clients and employers.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Skills Assessment" icon="award">
    Track and display your proficiency in various web technologies, from frontend frameworks to backend databases. The platform categorizes skills for easy navigation.

    <Expandable title="Skill Categories" default-open="false">
      Skills are organized into categories like frontend, backend, databases, and tools. This helps visitors quickly understand your expertise areas.
    </Expandable>

    <ExpandableGroup>
      <Expandable title="Frontend Skills" default-open="true">
        HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, and responsive design techniques.
      </Expandable>
      <Expandable title="Backend Skills" default-open="false">
        Node.js, Python, PHP, REST APIs, and database management.
      </Expandable>
    </ExpandableGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Experience Timeline" icon="calendar">
    Present your professional journey with a chronological timeline of roles, achievements, and key projects. Include company details and duration of employment.

    <Callout kind="info">The experience section builds credibility by showing your career progression and industry experience.</Callout>
  </Tab>
